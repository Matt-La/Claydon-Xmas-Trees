About Our Farm

Claydon Christmas Tree Farm is based in Steeple Claydon, between Buckingham and Bicester, approximately 20 minutes from Aylesbury. Claydon Christmas Tree Farm is managed by husband and wife team, Joseph and Ingrid Hodges. Being a family business that is run from our home, we take a real pride in the service that we give our customers.

We are primarily a working arable farm but decided to combine a farmer's enthusiasm for growing things with his wife's passion for Christmas. We aim to supply beautiful, freshly cut trees at sensible prices.

We planted our own trees in April 2010 and continue to plant Christmas trees every year. We began selling a few of our own trees in 2014 and hope to continue each year. The trees that we currently sell have been carefully selected from a farm in the Welsh Mountains. They have been cut only a few days before you purchase them and are from 3ft to 14ft tall, potted or fresh cut and all with that wonderful Christmas fragrance.

We are open from Saturday 28th November 2016 at 10am and everyday Monday to Friday 10am until 6pm, Saturday and Sunday 10am until 5pm until all of our Christmas Trees have been sold.
