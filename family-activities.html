Family Activities

Fun, fun, fun ...

While this area does not look as interesting as the reindeer or as exciting as the Christmas shop, the children's pedal tractor area is the highlight for every child and young family! This area has became a firm favourite, everyone delights in playing with the tractors in the straw bale enclosed area. Even more fun is gathering the straw and corn then carrying it to put into the trailers then emptying it again...and again....and again......and again.......

Free craft activities including icing gingerbread, face painting and other things for charity from 12pm until 3pm on Sat 26th & Sun 27th November, Sat & Sun 3rd, 4th, 10th and 11th December 2016.

Remember to come dressed appropriately, probably a good idea not to wear your best clothes!

Our refreshments area is situated opposite the tractor area so that parents can enjoy a well earned break while watching their children at the same time!

The tractor area is open at all times. Please supervise your children.
